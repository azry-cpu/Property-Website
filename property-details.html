About This Property

A masterpiece of modern architecture nestled in the prestigious hills of Beverly Hills. The Golden Estate offers an unparalleled living experience, combining state-of-the-art amenities with breathtaking panoramic views of the city skyline.

Upon entering through the grand pivot door, you are greeted by an expansive open-concept living area bathed in natural light, thanks to floor-to-ceiling automated glass walls that seamlessly blend indoor and outdoor living. The gourmet chef's kitchen features custom imported Italian cabinetry, top-of-the-line Miele appliances, and a stunning double-waterfall marble island.

The master suite is a true sanctuary, boasting a private terrace, a cozy fireplace, dual boutique-style walk-in closets, and an opulent spa-inspired bathroom with a freestanding soaking tub and dual rainfall showers. Step outside to your private oasis, complete with a zero-edge infinity pool, a submerged fire pit lounging area, and an outdoor kitchen perfect for entertaining under the California stars.
